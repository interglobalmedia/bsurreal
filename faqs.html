bsurreal FAQs

  1. º The game starts at the page you enter into from the Privacy/Terms and Conditions Page. To access the beginning of the game, click here.

  2. º Each time after you answer a question, a line of text will return moving your question location forward 1 number. For example, if you have just started the game and have only answered one question, you will be prompted with "Question 1 of X". After answering two questions, you will be prompted with "Question 2 of X", and so on.

  3. º When you are done answering the questions assigned to your page location, click on the "next round" button. It will take you to the next round of questions.

  4. º When you are done with the quiz portion of the games, you can continue onto the "Did you know?" portion of bsurreal.
